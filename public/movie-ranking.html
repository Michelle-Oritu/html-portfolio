Michelle's Movie Note

Top 3 movies that will change how you think

The Matrix

This film explores existential questions about reality, identity, and the nature of perception. It challenges viewers to consider whether the world they perceive is truly real or merely a constructed illusion, prompting deep introspection about the nature of existence and the power of choice.

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

This romantic science-fiction drama delves into the complexities of memory, love, and human relationships. Through its inventive narrative structure and emotional depth, it invites viewers to contemplate the significance of past experiences and the role they play in shaping our identities and connections with others.

Fight Club

Adapted from Chuck Palahniuk's novel, this film critiques consumerism, masculinity, and societal norms. It challenges viewers to question the pursuit of material wealth and societal expectations, while also exploring themes of identity, alienation, and the search for meaning in a modern world.
